Farm Profiles

Meet the farmers cultivating the Cuyahoga Valley

Explore each participating farm with quick visuals, locations, and links. We paired every profile with its logo and a scene from the farm to help you recognize them at the market or on a visit.

Trapp Family Farm

Sustainable mixed crop-and-livestock family farm powered by draft horses since 2012.

Farm Location
1019 West Streetsboro Road, Peninsula
Website
trappfamilyfarm.com
  • Seasonal eggs, produce, and meat are sold on-site.
  • Draft horses provide power across the farm's rotations.
  • Focused on resilient, healthy people, plants, animals, and soil.

Purple Brown Farmstead

A permaculture-oriented "food forest" blending diverse crops with complementary animals.

Shop Location
1619 Mill St. W, Peninsula
Farm Location
458 Hines Hill Road, Hudson
Website
purplebrownfarmstead.com
  • Heritage cider apples, fruits, nuts, vegetables, herbs, flowers, and mushrooms.
  • Integrated animal systems that complement the food forest.
  • Products also available at the downtown Peninsula farm store.

The Spicy Lamb Farm

A 12-acre working sheep farm and orchard in the Cuyahoga Valley National Park.

Farm Location
6560 Akron Peninsula Road
Website
thespicylamb.com
  • Purebred Dorset sheep for gourmet lamb and wool, plus free-range ducks and bees.
  • Orchard-grown organic heirloom fruits and vegetables.
  • Agritourism hub with sheepdog trials, fiber arts workshops, and Adopt-a-Sheep programs by appointment.

Oxbow Orchard

Diversified, regenerative agriculture with orchard fruit, pasture poultry, and flowers.

Farm Location
6765 Canal Road, Valley View
Website
oxboworchard.com
  • Icelandic sheep, pasture-raised chickens and turkeys.
  • Seasonal fruits, vegetables, seedlings, and cut flowers.
  • Rustic farmstand and small local foods shop on-site.

Greenfield Berry Farm

Pick-your-own berries, sunflowers, and a vegetable CSA led by educator Daniel Greenfield.

Farm Location
2485 Major Road, Peninsula
Website
greenfieldberryfarm.com
  • Pick-your-own blueberries, aronia berries, and sunflowers.
  • Vegetable CSA plus pussy willows, tulips, and honey.
  • Farm-based educational tours and field trips.
    Read a recent feature

Keleman Point Farm

Diversified sustainable farmstead continuing the legacy of the former Goatfeathers Point Farm.

Farm Location
570 Akron Peninsula Road
Website
kelemanpointfarm.com
  • Grass-fed beef, pork, heritage-breed turkeys, and Tennessee fainting goats for cashmere fiber.
  • Free-range eggs and seasonal meats offered year-round.
  • Farm Market stand serves the community across all seasons.

Sarah's Vineyard

A vineyard, winery, tasting room, and art gallery operated by the Lytz family.

Vineyard & Market
1204 West Steels Corners Road, Cuyahoga Falls
Farm Location
1274 West Steels Corners Road, Cuyahoga Falls
Website
sarahsvineyardwinery.com
  • Multiple grape varieties and nearly a dozen wines, including an estate wine.
  • Indoor/outdoor dining, a tasting room, live music, and gallery experiences.
  • Hosts the annual Summer Solstice Wine, Art, & Music Festival each June.

Spice Acres

Chef-driven farming linking harvests to Spice Catering Co. and Spice Field Kitchen.

Farm Location
9570 Riverview Road, Brecksville
Website
spiceacres.com
  • Seasonal produce and heritage pork grown for Spice's culinary teams.
  • Direct connection between chefs and field planning.
  • Farm-to-table dinners and workshops hosted on-site.

Neitenbach Farm

Biodynamic vegetables, herbs, and flowers grown alongside holistic healing services.

Farm Location
3077 Akron Peninsula Road, Akron
Social
@theneitenbachfarm
  • Mixed vegetables, culinary and medicinal herbs, and flowers.
  • On-farm stand and a distinctive CSA.
  • Herbal tinctures, teas, salves, and holistic bodywork led by Pamela Neitenbach.

Preserving farmland and supporting local agriculture in the Cuyahoga Valley.
